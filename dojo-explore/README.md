# DojoExplore: Biotechnology Workshop Program

**Macalester College Nucleate Chapter Leadership Project**

A comprehensive 6-week biotechnology workshop program that connected 30+ Macalester College students with 10+ industry professionals and alumni. This project demonstrates leadership in program management, professional development coordination, and educational outreach.

## 📋 Overview

DojoExplore was an initiative by the Macalester Nucleate Chapter to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry practice in biotechnology. Through structured seminars, networking, and hands-on learning, we provided students with insider perspectives on biotech careers and research.

**Impact:** 30+ students engaged | 10+ industry professionals | $2,500 budget managed | 6 weeks of programming

## 🎯 Program Objectives

1. **Educate** students about biotechnology career paths
2. **Connect** students with industry professionals
3. **Inspire** the next generation of biotech innovators
4. **Build** professional networks between students and industry
5. **Gather data** to inform future programming

## 📈 Analysis Results

### Attendance Trends
```
Week 1: 28 → Week 2: 26 → Week 3: 24 → Week 4: 22 → Week 5: 31 → Week 6: 20
```
*Note: Week 5 spike due to professional development + evening event*

### Feedback Highlights
**What students loved:**
- "Real-world perspectives from professionals" (94%)
- "Networking opportunities" (87%)
- "Diverse career path examples" (91%)
- "Professional development focus" (82%)

**Suggestions for improvement:**
- More time for Q&A (requested by 23%)
- Recorded sessions for those who missed (18%)
- More focus on diversity in biotech (14%)

## 💡 Lessons Learned & Recommendations

### What Worked Well
1. ✅ Industry speaker recruitment was successful
2. ✅ Student interest was high
3. ✅ Data collection was manageable
4. ✅ Budget was well-allocated
5. ✅ Week 5 attendance spike with professional development

### Areas for Improvement
1. 📌 Maintain momentum (slight decline mid-program)
2. 📌 Consider hybrid attendance options
3. 📌 Expand speaker diversity (add more women/underrepresented groups)
4. 📌 Create follow-up job/internship matching
5. 📌 Record sessions for asynchronous access

### For Future Iterations
- Expand to 8 weeks for more depth
- Add hands-on lab experience if possible
- Create alumni mentor matching program
- Build job board for opportunities
- Increase speaker honorariums to attract more senior-level professionals
